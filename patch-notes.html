Patch Notes

A running history of what's changed on this website, version by version.

v4.2.0 Current
  • Removed the separate Contact page. Every "Contact" link and button across the site now points to our Request Service form instead -- one clear way to reach out, rather than two separate forms doing the same job. Calling or texting 804-309-0968 directly always works too.
  • The homepage testimonial carousel now grows and shrinks smoothly to fit each review as it rotates, instead of resizing instantly.
  • The "Learn more" link under our testimonials now only appears alongside Bill Armour's review, since it points to his specific project in our Portfolio.
  • Updated our Google Business link and added Nextdoor to the platforms we're present on, shown in the site footer.
v4.1.0
  • Requesting service is now a lot simpler. Our project form used to ask a long list of questions up front, with instructions to type "N/A" for anything that didn't apply -- based on real customer feedback, it now just asks for your name, email, phone, preferred contact method, and a short description of what you need. After submitting, you land on a dedicated confirmation page instead of just an in-page message.
  • Added a rotating testimonial carousel to the homepage, including two new five-star Google reviews, alongside our existing customer testimonial.
  • Linked the service list on the homepage (Websites, Computer repair, Networking, Cybersecurity, Small business IT) directly to each service's dedicated page.
  • Removed the animated "drag to compare" before/after website demo from the Services page -- simplified in favor of a cleaner page.
  • Expanded our networking services to include MoCA (coax) networking, home ethernet installation, and Starlink installation.
  • Fixed a real bug: our own contact, booking, newsletter, and project-request forms could fail to submit, silently, for every visitor. Replaced with a more reliable submission system.
  • Added a dedicated confirmation page for the Website Designer's quick-quote flow, usable as a direct link for ads and other campaigns.
v4.0.0
  • The entire site has been redesigned -- new typography, colors, spacing, and button styles throughout, both here and in the Care Hub customer portal, aimed at a cleaner and more polished look and feel.
  • Removed the light/dark mode toggle. The site now uses one clean, consistently-designed light theme everywhere instead of maintaining two.
  • This site is now English only. The 16-language switcher has been removed; your browser's own built-in translation feature will still work on this content if you need it.
v3.3.0
  • Website Designer now hands your full project details to a dedicated worksheet, opened in its own tab.   • Fixed a real bug: on Safari, that new tab could look like it failed to open even when it had actually opened correctly. Safari treats a certain browser instruction as a signal to hide the result from the page that opened it, and this tool was relying on that same result to decide whether to show a "your browser blocked this" fallback message -- so every Safari visitor saw that fallback, whether or not the tab really opened. Fixed so Safari now reports it accurately
  • Replaced the plain-text project summary PDF with a fully redesigned, professionally branded document -- a proper cover page, clearly itemized pricing, and your submitted business details laid out across as many pages as needed

Featured Project

Armour Wireless Solutions, LLC

A professional credibility website built for a specialized wireless consulting company serving carrier, partner, and government-facing audiences.

Industry

Wireless consulting and infrastructure support

Project Type

Responsive business website

Expanded Scope

Secure photo-management tools

Armour Wireless needed a clear, professional online presence that could explain the company, present relevant experience and capabilities, organize approved contracting information, and give business contacts one dependable link to share.

1. Custom responsive design

Desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts built around Armour Wireless branding and a professional, Navy-inspired direction.

2. Business content structure

Home, company background, capabilities, contracting information, contact content, and clear navigation.

3. Copy and presentation support

Client-provided information organized into readable, web-ready content for carrier and government-facing visitors.

4. Launch coordination

Deployment support, domain coordination, click-to-call and click-to-email functions, testing, and revisions.

5. Secure administrator access

A protected client sign-in process with two-factor authentication and controlled access to management functions.

6. Client-managed photo gallery

Responsive public gallery plus image upload and removal tools, storage integration, validation, and handoff guidance.

Delivered outcome: A clean, mobile-ready website that gives Armour Wireless one professional destination for its company story, capabilities, contracting information, contact options, and client-managed project photos — without requiring the client to edit code.

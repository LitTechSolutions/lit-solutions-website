The founder

Veteran-owned. Mission-trained.

Little Technical Solutions LLC is proudly veteran-owned. Founder Dylan Little served six years in the U.S. Navy as a Fire Controlman, including four years aboard the guided-missile cruiser USS Antietam, maintaining high-voltage combat systems and command-and-control electronics — hands-on troubleshooting, repair, and testing where equipment failure wasn't an option. That same discipline and attention to detail now goes into every website, network, and repair job. We extend that same appreciation to others who serve — see our American Heroes Discount.

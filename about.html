The founder

Veteran-owned. Mission-trained.

Little Technical Solutions LLC is proudly veteran-owned. Founder Dylan Little served six years in the U.S. Navy as a Fire Controlman, including four years aboard the guided-missile cruiser USS Antietam, maintaining high-voltage combat systems and command-and-control electronics — hands-on troubleshooting, repair, and testing where equipment failure wasn't an option. After the Navy, Dylan carried that same discipline into a career as a systems engineer while earning a Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity Technology. He founded Little Technical Solutions to put that technical background to work for local businesses — building websites and handling IT the way he'd want it done for his own. We extend that same appreciation to others who serve — see our American Heroes Discount.

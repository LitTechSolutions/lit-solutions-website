General Website Projects Pricing & Payment

General

What areas do you serve?

Computer repair, networking, and on-site cybersecurity work is available in person across Montross, Colonial Beach, King George, and Dahlgren, Virginia. Website design is different — it's fully remote, so we take on website clients anywhere in the United States. See our Service Area page for the full breakdown.

How do I get started?

Call 636-426-0289, email dylan@lit-solutions.tech, or fill out our project form — we typically respond the same business day.

Do you sign a contract?

Yes — our Terms & Conditions cover payment terms, cancellations, ownership, and everything else in plain language before any project starts.

Website Projects

What's the difference between the Starter and Business website packages?

Starter covers up to 5 pages with the essentials — contact form, mobile-responsive design, and basic SEO. Business covers up to 10 pages plus advanced features like individual service pages, booking, testimonials, FAQ, and a blog. See the full package comparison for the exact breakdown.

How long does a website project take?

It depends on scope, but most Starter sites are completed in 1–2 weeks and Business sites in 2–4 weeks once we have your content and any photos. Timelines are confirmed upfront before work begins.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — one revision round is included on the Starter package and two on the Business package, so you can request changes before final launch.

What happens after my site launches?

You get 30 days of post-launch support on Starter and 60 days on Business. After that, ongoing maintenance and hosting assistance is available as a monthly subscription if you want it.

I'm not in Virginia — can you still build my site?

Yes. Website design is 100% remote from first call to launch. Your location doesn't matter for this service.

Pricing & Payment

How does payment work?

We confirm your exact price before any work begins, then you pay through our secure Square payment page. Recurring services (subscriptions) are billed monthly through Square as well.

What is the American Heroes Discount?

A 15% discount on one-time services (5% on recurring/subscription services) for active duty military, veterans, teachers, first responders, doctors, TSA agents, police, firefighters, and Virginia FFL holders. See Heroes Discount for eligibility and verification details.

Are your prices the final price?

The prices on our pricing page are accurate starting points for typical jobs. Parts, extensive repairs, and larger custom projects are quoted individually and always confirmed with you before work begins — no surprise line items.

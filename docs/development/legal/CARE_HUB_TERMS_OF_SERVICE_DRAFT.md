## LTS Business Care Hub — Terms of Service

*Governs your use of the Care Hub account and portal
(`/care-hub/`). This is separate from any general terms of use for the
public lit-solutions.tech website.*

### 1. Accounts

The Care Hub is invitation-only at launch — you cannot self-register.
Your account is created by an invitation from Little Technical
Solutions LLC or from an administrator within your organization. You're
responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential and for
all activity under your account. Administrator ("platform_admin")
accounts are required to enable two-factor authentication.

### 2. Roles and access

Care Hub accounts belong to an organization and have one of a small set
of roles (owner, member, read-only, or LTS staff/admin), each with
different access to that organization's data. You only have access to
your own organization's records — Little Technical Solutions LLC staff
have broader access solely to deliver support and services to you.

### 3. Acceptable use

You agree to use the Care Hub only for its intended purpose — managing
your account, support tickets, checklists, and related records with
Little Technical Solutions LLC. You agree not to attempt to access
another organization's data, probe or attack the platform's security,
upload unlawful or harmful content, or use the Care Hub in any way that
violates applicable law.

### 4. Content you submit

You retain ownership of the files, messages, and information you submit
through the Care Hub. You grant us the right to store, process, and
display that content back to you and to our staff solely to provide the
service. You're responsible for making sure you have the right to
upload whatever you submit.

### 5. Support tickets and service delivery

Submitting a ticket or request through the Care Hub does not by itself
create or modify any service agreement, scope of work, or pricing
between you and Little Technical Solutions LLC — those are governed by
whatever separate engagement or scope-of-work document applies to your
organization. The Care Hub is a communication and tracking tool for
that underlying relationship, not a replacement for it.

### 6. Payments

*(Placeholder pending Square integration going live — see
`OWNER_DECISIONS.md` for outstanding pricing/deposit policy decisions.)*
Where the Care Hub is used to request or track payment, actual payment
is processed by our payment processor under its own terms; we do not
collect or store your card details.

### 7. Availability

We aim to keep the Care Hub available but do not guarantee
uninterrupted access. We may perform maintenance, and we may suspend or
terminate an account that violates these terms or poses a security
risk to the platform or other organizations' data.

### 8. Termination

You or we may end your access to the Care Hub at any time. If your
organization's relationship with Little Technical Solutions LLC ends,
your account access ends with it, subject to the data retention periods
described in the Care Hub Privacy Policy.
